birthDate

BirthDate of a person.

BirthDateCredentialV1

BirthDateCredentialV1 is a type extension of credential subject into VC.

BirthDatePerson

BirthDate of a person.

Property Type Required Notes
"@type" BirthDatePerson true
birthDate datetime ture
